# Financial Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and track key financial metrics, sales trends, and product-wise profitability. 

## 📊 Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard Screenshot](Screenshot 2026-06-19 162514.png)
*(Note: Agar aapne screenshot ka naam badla hai, toh yahan wahi naam likhein)*

## 🚀 Key Features & Insights
* **Executive Summary KPIs:** High-level tracking of Gross Sales ($128M), Total Sales ($119M), Net Profit ($17M), and total Units Sold (1.13M).
* **Sales & Profit Trends:** A comprehensive timeline chart analyzing Gross Sales vs. Net Profit across different months.
* **Geographical Performance:** Identification of the 'Most Profitable Countries' (led by France and Germany).
* **Segment Analysis:** A donut chart showing the Sales Volume Contribution broken down by market segments.
* **Product Performance Breakdown:** A detailed granular table mapping Units Sold, Profit ($USD), and Discount Bands across various products like Amarilla and Carretera.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
* **Power BI Desktop:** For data modeling, DAX calculations, and data visualization.
* **Dataset:** Financial sample dataset containing sales and profit records.
